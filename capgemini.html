Capgemini recuirtment process

Capgemini conducts 4 rounds to select freshers as Software Engineer in their organization

  • Aptitude and Essay Round
  • Technical Aptitude Round
  • Technical Round
  • HR Round

Academic Criteria:

65 percent or above in B.Tech, Class X and XII.
No backlogs at the time of interview

Aptitude and Essay Round:

Aptitude round consists of 3 sections namely Quantitative reasoning, logical reasoning and essay writing section. This round is generally conducted on Cocubes.

Technical Aptitude Round:

The students who clear the quantity round are called for Technical Aptitude Round. To clear this round you should be clear with your basics. You should be prepared with Data structures and Algorithms, DBMS, Operating System.

Technical Round:

To clear this round you should be clear with your basics. You should be prepared with Data structures and Algorithms, DBMS, Operating System. Students will be expected to write codes in the interview. They also ask questions from resume. They frequently ask questions about your previous projects. You may be asked puzzles in this round. To be prepared for puzzles you can practice from our Puzzles section.
